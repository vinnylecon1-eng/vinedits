## 3. Niche Analysis

### What is Niche Analysis?

Niche analysis is the process of understanding your specific audience, content style, and optimal posting patterns. It's the foundation for all content generation and auto-posting.

### Run Niche Analysis

1. Go to **Niche Analysis** page
2. Click **Analyze My Niche**
3. Select the account to analyze
4. Wait 2-3 minutes for analysis to complete

### What You'll Get

The analysis provides:

**Audience Demographics**
- Age range
- Gender distribution
- Geographic location
- Interests and behaviors

**Content Analysis**
- Primary theme (your niche)
- Sub-themes (specific topics)
- Content pillars (core content types)
- Unique angle (what makes you different)

**Engagement Patterns**
- Peak engagement hours
- Peak engagement days
- Best performing content types
- Hashtag performance

**Optimal Posting Times**
- Best time to post Monday-Sunday
- Platform-specific recommendations
- Timezone-aware scheduling

**Competitor Analysis**
- Top 5 competitors in your niche
- Their content strategy
- Their posting schedule
- Content gaps you can fill

### Understanding Your Niche Profile

**Example: Fitness Niche**

```
Primary Theme: Fitness & Health
Sub-themes: Weight Loss, Muscle Building, Nutrition
Unique Angle: Fitness for busy professionals (15-min workouts)

Content Pillars:
- Workout Videos (40%)
- Nutrition Tips (25%)
- Transformation Stories (20%)
- Q&A/Educational (10%)
- Behind-the-Scenes (5%)

Peak Engagement:
- Days: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- Times: 12pm, 6pm, 8pm
- Best Content: Reels (85% engagement)

Optimal Posting Schedule:
- Monday: 12pm, 6pm
- Tuesday: 12pm, 6pm, 8pm
- Wednesday: 12pm, 6pm, 8pm
- Thursday: 9am, 12pm, 6pm
- Friday: 3pm (low engagement)
- Saturday-Sunday: Stories only
```

### Update Your Niche Profile

Your niche profile updates automatically every 30 days. You can also manually refresh:

1. Go to **Niche Analysis**
2. Click **Refresh Profile**
3. Wait for analysis to complete

## 4. Content Generation

### Generate Viral Content

Once you've analyzed your niche, generate optimized content:

1. Go to **Content Generator**
2. Click **Generate Content**
3. Select platform (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Facebook, All)
4. Click **Generate**
5. Wait 30-60 seconds for AI to generate content

### What Gets Generated

For each video/post, you'll get:

**6 Viral Hooks**
- Hook Archetype (Fortuneteller, Experimenter, Teacher, Magician, Investigator, Contrarian)
- Visual Description
- On-Screen Text
- Verbal Opening
- Engagement Score (0-100)

**5 SEO-Optimized Titles**
- Under 60 characters
- Includes primary keyword
- Creates curiosity or benefit
- Platform-specific

**3 Keyword-Rich Descriptions**
- Platform-specific
- Includes relevant keywords
- Natural language (not keyword stuffing)
- Call-to-action included

**Hashtag Strategy**
- Broad hashtags (high volume)
- Niche hashtags (specific audience)
- Trending hashtags (current trends)
- Total: 8-12 hashtags

**Engaging Captions**
- Emoji-enhanced
- Engagement-focused
- Call-to-action
- Matches your brand voice

**Virality Score**
- Overall score (0-100)
- Component scores (hook, title, hashtags, caption)
- Improvement suggestions

### Hook Archetypes Explained

#### 1. Fortuneteller 🔮
Hints at transformation or result.
- **Best for:** Before/after content, transformations
- **Example:** "I quit social media for 30 days - here's what happened"
- **Engagement:** 82/100

#### 2. Experimenter 🧪
Shows curiosity through testing.
- **Best for:** Experiments, challenges, tests
- **Example:** "I posted 3 times daily for a week - here are the results"
- **Engagement:** 78/100

#### 3. Teacher 📚
Promises fast, useful value.
- **Best for:** Educational content, tips, tutorials
- **Example:** "3 proven strategies to boost your engagement"
- **Engagement:** 75/100

#### 4. Magician ✨
Uses visual surprise and editing.
- **Best for:** Transformation videos, editing showcases
- **Example:** "This editing trick gets millions of views"
- **Engagement:** 88/100

#### 5. Investigator 🔍
Teases hidden truth or insider knowledge.
- **Best for:** Revealing secrets, industry insights
- **Example:** "No one talks about this but..."
- **Engagement:** 85/100

#### 6. Contrarian 🔥
Flips popular belief with bold opinion.
- **Best for:** Opinion pieces, controversial takes
- **Example:** "Everything you know is wrong"
- **Engagement:** 80/100

### Edit Generated Content

You can edit any generated content before posting:

1. Click on the content you want to edit
2. Make changes to titles, hashtags, captions
3. Click **Save Changes**
4. Changes are saved for this post

### Regenerate Content

Don't like the generated content? Regenerate:

1. Click **Regenerate**
2. AI creates new variations
3. Choose your favorite

## 5. Auto-Posting

### Schedule Posts

Schedule generated content to post automatically:

1. Go to **Content Generator**
2. Generate content
3. Click **Schedule Post**
4. Select platform
5. Choose posting time (or use AI recommendation)
6. Click **Schedule**

### Optimal Posting Times

The system automatically calculates optimal posting times based on:
- Your niche analysis
- Your audience's behavior
- Platform-specific patterns
- Day of week
- Time of day

### Scheduled Posts

View and manage all scheduled posts:

1. Go to **Scheduled Posts**
2. See all upcoming posts
3. Edit or delete posts
4. View posting history

### Auto-Posting Rules

The system automatically:
- Posts at scheduled time
- Distributes posts throughout week
- Avoids posting same time consecutively
- Mixes content types
- Respects platform best practices

### Posting History

After posts are published:
- Timestamp of posting
- Platform posted to
- Initial engagement metrics
- Status (posted, failed, pending)

## 7. Best Practices

### Content Strategy

**Follow Your Content Pillars**
- Stick to 3-5 core content types
- Maintain 60-70% consistent content
- 20-30% experimental content
- 10-20% engagement/community content

**Post Consistently**
- Use auto-posting for consistency
- Post 3-5 times per week (platform dependent)
- Maintain regular schedule
- Avoid posting same time consecutively

**Engage with Your Audience**
- Respond to comments
- Answer questions
- Like and comment on similar content
- Build community

### Hook Strategy

**Use Different Hooks**
- Don't repeat same hook
- Rotate through all 6 archetypes
- Test which hooks work best for your niche
- Adapt based on performance

**Hook Best Practices**
- First 3 seconds are critical
- Hook should match content
- Visual hook should be clear
- Text hook should be readable

### Hashtag Strategy

**Use Recommended Hashtags**
- Follow the AI-generated hashtag mix
- 2-3 broad hashtags (high volume)
- 3-5 niche hashtags (specific audience)
- 2-3 trending hashtags (current trends)

**Hashtag Research**
- Check hashtag volume
- Look at competitor hashtags
- Test new hashtags
- Track hashtag performance

### Caption Strategy

**Engagement-Focused Captions**
- Ask questions
- Include call-to-action
- Use emoji strategically
- Keep it conversational

**Caption Length**
- Instagram: 125-150 characters optimal
- TikTok: 150-200 characters optimal
- YouTube: 200-300 characters optimal
- LinkedIn: 300-400 characters optimal

### Posting Schedule

**Optimal Days**
- Best: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- Good: Monday, Friday
- Avoid: Saturday, Sunday

**Optimal Times**
- Lunch break: 11am-1pm
- Afternoon: 3pm-5pm
- Evening: 6pm-9pm
- Avoid: Early morning, late night
